Season Summary & Analysis

Championship Story

RCB's maiden title after 18 seasons was the defining moment of IPL 2025. Their emotional journey from perennial underachievers to champions captured hearts worldwide.

Gujarat Titans dominated individual awards with Sai Sudharsan (Orange Cap, Emerging Player) and Prasidh Krishna (Purple Cap), while Suryakumar Yadav claimed MVP honors.
